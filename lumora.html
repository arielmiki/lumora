Lumora

Cinematic product video, generated.

The brand system for an AI film studio built for modern commerce — warm, luminous and quietly premium.

01 — The name

Why “Lumora”

Lumora draws from lumen (light) and aura — the glow a product carries when it's shot beautifully. It speaks to what the studio does: it makes every product luminous.

The word is coined and ownable, soft enough to feel premium and DTC-native, yet short and confident. It reads as a heritage atelier and a modern AI tool at once — pronounced loo-MOR-ah.

Naming conventions extend to the plans — Atelier, Studio, Maison — and to outputs, which are called films, never “clips.”

02 — Logo & mark

The aperture mark

A hexagonal camera aperture with a luminous core — light, optics and craft in a single form. The wordmark is set in Fraunces, all-caps, with generous tracking for an editorial, fashion-house cadence.

03 — Color

Espresso & champagne

A warm, low-light palette that lets gold do the talking. Backgrounds stay deep and warm; champagne gold is the single hero accent, with mauve, eucalyptus and clay reserved for data and detail.

04 — Typography

Fraunces & Hanken Grotesk

An editorial serif with optical warmth, paired with a clean, humanist grotesque. Serif for voice and emotion; grotesque for clarity and interface.
